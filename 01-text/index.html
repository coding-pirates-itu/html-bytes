Text formatting

  1. html, head, body
  2. lang in the html
  3. title for page title
  4. h1-h6 for headers
  5. p for paragraph; spaces and newlines are ignored
  6. br for line breaks
  7. em for italic
  8. strong for bold
  9. a for links
  10. sub for textsubscript
  11. sup for textsuperscript
  12. <-- / --> for comments
